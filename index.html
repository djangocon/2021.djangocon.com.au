DjangoConAU

DjangoCon AU is an annual gathering of Django developers in Australia.

DjangoCon AU has run for the past eight years as a specialist track under PyCon AU, started by Russell Keith-Magee in 2013, and run annually by a very small number of rotating volunteers.

DjangoCon AU will not be running in 2021, but we encourage Django enthusiasts to attend other Django events:

DjangoCon Europe plans to run in June, see https://2021.djangocon.eu/
DjangoCon US plans to run in October, see https://2021.djangocon.us/

We also encourage Australian-based Djangonauts to attend PyCon AU, which is running online in 2021: http://2021.pycon.org.au/


Stay safe and well,
