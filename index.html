DjangoConAU 2021?

DjangoCon AU is an annual gathering of Django developers in Australia. It has been held since 2013, and it needs your help to run again.

DjangoCon AU has run for the past eight years as a specialist track under PyCon AU, started by Russell Keith-Magee in 2013, and run annually by a very small number of rotating volunteers.

As interests and the ability to volunteer change, we are seeking members of the community who want to organise DjangoCon AU 2021. DjangoCon AU will not run this year without your help.

Our parent conference PyCon AU has an open call for specialist tracks, and we want to know who is interested in taking up the mantle in 2021.

For more information, see the PyCon AU call for specialist tacks announcement here: https://2021.pycon.org.au/tracks/. Submissions close March 31, 2021

DjangoCon still happens at least once annually throughout the world, and has since 2008. A lack of Australian event in 2021 is not the end of the Django community, as there are other events already planned to run virtually this year:

DjangoCon Europe plans to run in June, see https://2021.djangocon.eu/
DjangoCon US plans to run in October, see https://2021.djangocon.us/

If you are interested in making DjangoCon AU 2021 happen, please get in touch with Katie at katie@djangocon.com.au. She is open to discussing the logistics involved, and to help review submission statements.
